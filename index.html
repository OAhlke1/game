What's this game about?

You have to collect all three ammo-crystals to get the special shoot
so that you can defeat the main boss.

Controls:
go left: press left key or touch the left side of the gaming screen.
go left: press right key or touch the right side of the gaming screen.
run: press SHIFT or touch the running-button.
jump: press the spacebar or touch the center of the gaming screen.
shoot: press d or touch the bottom of the gaming screen.
